Key Features of GrocerySnap

  • Barcode Scanner: Instantly scan products to add them to your grocery list.
  • Real-Time List: Items show up immediately after scanning, no manual entry needed.
  • Quantity Control: Easily increase or decrease quantities of each item.
  • Delete Items: Remove unnecessary items with one click.
  • User Authentication: Keep your data secure with login and signup functionality using Firebase.
  • Mobile Responsive: Works great on both desktop and mobile screens.
